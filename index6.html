Today car sharing movements have appeared all over the world. In many cities,

car sharing has made a strong impact on how city residents travel. ① Even in strong

car-ownership cultures such as North America, car sharing has gained popularity.

② In the U.S. and Canada, membership in car sharing now exceeds one in five adults

in many urban areas. ③ Strong influence on traffic jams and pollution can be felt

from Toronto to New York, as each shared vehicle replaces around 10 personal cars.

④ The best thing about driverless cars is that people won't need a license to operate

them. ⑤ City governments with downtown areas struggling with traffic jams and lack of
